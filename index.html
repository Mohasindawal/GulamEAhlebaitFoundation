Our Core Initiatives

The Gulam-e-Ahlebait Foundation is dedicated to addressing pressing societal challenges through focused and impactful programs. We believe in the fundamental rights of education and health for all.

Education for All

We provide financial assistance, distribute books and uniforms, and host educational workshops to ensure underprivileged children have access to quality learning.

Empowering Futures Through Education

Healthcare Access

From operating a low-cost clinic to providing essential medications and facilitating emergency ambulance transport, we ensure vital medical care reaches the needy.

Healing Hands, Healthy Communities

Humanitarian Aid & Welfare

We offer essential clothes, food items, monthly ration kits, and blankets to those in need, along with rent assistance, helping families rebuild their lives.

Sustaining Lives, Restoring Dignity

Livelihood & Empowerment

Our income generation initiatives, including microloans, skill development programs, and job placement services, empower individuals to achieve long-term success.

Fostering Self-Reliance

Charitable Hospital Initiative

Proud to announce the recent opening of our charitable hospital in Mumbra, providing affordable and essential medical services to the community.

Our Charitable Hospital, Mumbra

Special Charitable Drives

We organize Eid ul Fitr & Ramzan Charity Kit Distribution and coordinate Collective Qurbani, ensuring festive joy and essential support for families.

Annual Charity & Festive Drives

About Gulam-e-Ahlebait Foundation

Established in 2021, Gulam-e-Ahlebait Foundation is dedicated to improving educational opportunities for underprivileged children and providing essential medical assistance and humanitarian aid to the needy. Inspired by the teachings of Ahlebait, we are committed to serving humanity with compassion and justice, believing that 'the hands which give are bigger than the hands which take.
